Professional Hockeys Club

A professional hockey club is a team that competes in a league where players are salaried athletes. These teams typically play a fast-paced, physical game on ice using sticks to maneuver a puck and score goals on a netted goalpost. They are a source of entertainment and community pride, often boasting passionate fan bases and storied histories.
